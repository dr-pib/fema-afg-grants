## 2. Eligible activities (what FP&S funds)

Two distinct activities. Overview: https://www.fema.gov/grants/preparedness/firefighters/safety-awards and https://www.fema.gov/grants/preparedness/firefighters/safety-awards/research-development

Per FEMA: grants support "(1) activities designed to reach high-risk target groups and mitigate incidences of death and injuries caused by fire and fire-related hazards ('Fire Prevention and Safety Activity') and (2) research and development activities aimed at improving firefighter safety ('Firefighter Safety Research and Development Activity')."

### (a) Fire Prevention and Safety (FP&S) Activity
Five project categories:
1. **Community Risk Reduction** — smoke alarm installations (door-to-door with home safety inspection), smoke alarms, sprinkler awareness, risk assessments, public education, training, juvenile firesetter intervention, general prevention; includes at-risk groups (deaf/hard of hearing, older adults, disabled).
2. **Wildfire Risk Reduction** — Wildland-Urban Interface (WUI) education/awareness, wood-chipper programs, external home sprinkler kits.
3. **Code Enforcement/Awareness** — first-time or reinstatement of code adoption/enforcement, building/fire code adoption or awareness, inspections or pre-planning.
4. **Fire Investigation (Origin and Cause)** — fire investigation activities.
5. **National/State/Regional Programs and Projects** — larger-scope programs across broader geographies (may qualify for a longer performance period).

**Eligible for the FP&S Activity:** fire departments (50 states, DC, territories, tribes) AND national/regional/state/local/tribal/nonprofit "interest organizations" recognized for fire-prevention/safety experience.
Sources: https://www.fema.gov/grants/preparedness/firefighters/safety-awards ; https://www.fema.gov/sites/default/files/documents/fema_rsl_gpd_fy25-fps-factsheet.05182026.pdf

### (b) Firefighter Safety Research and Development (R&D) Activity
Four project categories:
1. **Clinical Studies** — behavioral/social-science/cultural + physiological/medical research reducing fatal and non-fatal firefighter injuries.
2. **Technology and Product Development** — outcomes enhancing firefighter safety (e.g., situational awareness, fire phenomena).
3. **Preliminary Studies** — narrower-scope, short-duration, same scientific rigor.
4. **Early Career Investigator** — PI who earned a terminal degree or ended post-graduate training within 10 years of the application opening, and not previously a PI on an FP&S award.

**Eligible for R&D:** national/state/local/tribal/nonprofit organizations — including universities, research foundations, public-safety/public-health/occupational-health/injury-prevention institutions. **Fire departments are NOT eligible for R&D.** Individuals cannot receive awards (institution sponsors); every project needs at least one fire-service partner.
Source: https://www.fema.gov/grants/preparedness/firefighters/safety-awards/research-development

## 3. Key rules

### Cost share / match
- **5% non-federal cost share** for all applicants (cash and in-kind eligible). Cost Share Calculator provided.
- FY25 narrower waivers/exceptions: **not verified.**
Source: https://www.fema.gov/sites/default/files/documents/fema_rsl_gpd_fy25-fps-activity-faq_05182026.pdf

### Funding amounts (FY 2025)
- **$32.4M** appropriated for FP&S (Full-Year Continuing Appropriations and Extensions Act, 2025, §1101).
- **Maximum federal share per recipient: $1.5M** (§33(d)(2), Federal Fire Prevention and Control Act of 1974; 15 U.S.C. §2229(d)(2)) — total across up to six projects (up to 3 FP&S + up to 3 R&D per application).
Source: https://www.fema.gov/sites/default/files/documents/fema_rsl_gpd_fy25-fps-activity-faq_05182026.pdf

### Period of performance
- **FP&S Activity:** starts at award, generally **12 months**; complex (National/State/Regional) projects may request **up to 24 months**.
- **R&D Activity:** starts at award, **up to 48 months**.
Source: https://www.fema.gov/sites/default/files/documents/fema_rsl_gpd_fy25-fps-activity-faq_05182026.pdf

### Application evaluation criteria (FY 2025 FP&S Activity)
Weights differ for Fire Departments vs. Interest Organizations:

| Criterion | Fire Departments | Interest Organizations |
|---|---|---|
| Financial Need | 10% | 0% |
| Commitment to Hazard (Fire) Mitigation | 5% | n/a |
| Vulnerability Statement | 15% | 25% |
| Project Description | 20% | 25% |
| Implementation Plan | 25% | 30% |
| Evaluation Plan | 15% | 15% |
| Cost-Benefit | 10% | 5% |

- Submitted/managed in **FEMA GO**. Help: AFG Program Help Desk 866-274-0960 / firegrants@fema.dhs.gov.
- R&D Activity has its own narrative/criteria in the FY25 R&D Program Guide (specific R&D weights **not verified**).
Source: https://www.fema.gov/sites/default/files/documents/fema_rsl_gpd_fy25-fps-activity-application_05182026.pdf
